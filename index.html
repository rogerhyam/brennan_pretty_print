This utility will render a list of the CDs on you Brennan B3 by calling the /hdd1/b2db URL on your Brennan then rendering it in a prettier way. You can then print the page to have a physical index.

Security: This should be totally safe and private. Nothing is uploaded to the server. All the work is done in your browser. Your browser doesn't have permission to alter files on your computer or Brennan. This only works on your local networ where your Brennan is accessible.
